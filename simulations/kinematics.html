🏁 Projectile Motion

Grade XI • Kinematics

About This Simulation

This projectile motion simulator demonstrates kinematics in two dimensions. Adjust the launch angle and initial velocity to observe how the trajectory, range, maximum height, and time of flight change.

Range = v² sin(2θ) / g   |   H_max = v² sin²(θ) / 2g   |   T = 2v sin(θ) / g
